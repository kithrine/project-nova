# Security and Privacy

## Classification

- Public
- Internal operational
- Confidential personal
- Restricted case information
- Highly restricted

## Highly restricted examples

- Background reports
- Criminal-history details
- Government ID documents
- Serious incident investigations
- Sensitive audit records

## Controls

- Least privilege
- Organization scope
- Restricted queries
- Audit events
- Named exports
- No restricted global search
- No sensitive logging
- Secure object storage
- Environment separation

## Training records

Training status, dates, provider name, structured completion method, and verifier are internal operational data. Do not store assessment answers, medical details, restricted background information, or sensitive narratives in Training Enrollment evidence. Story 3.4 participant views receive only the rolled-up journey step.

## Legal review required

- Retention periods
- Background-check obligations
- Employment records
- Grant data requirements
- Participant access and correction rights
