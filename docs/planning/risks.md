# Risk Register

| Risk                                                          | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation                                                                                      |
| ------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------: | -----: | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Participant or animal safety incident                         |     Medium |   High | Screening, training, supervision, incident workflow, insurance                                  |
| Employer-of-record ambiguity                                  |     Medium |   High | Written agreements, explicit operating model, legal review                                      |
| Grants reimburse in arrears                                   |     Medium |   High | Working-capital plan, small cohort, milestone draws                                             |
| Grant expiration                                              |     Medium |   High | Outcomes, renewals, fee-for-service exploration                                                 |
| Shelter cold start                                            |     Medium | Medium | Hand-seed pilot partnerships                                                                    |
| Participant privacy breach                                    | Low/Medium |   High | Least privilege, restricted data, audit logs, scoped exports                                    |
| Overbuilt MVP                                                 |       High | Medium | Locked scope and deferred-feature list                                                          |
| Shared nonproduction DB interference                          |     Medium | Medium | Unique test data, targeted cleanup, no global truncation                                        |
| Legal assumptions incorrect                                   |     Medium |   High | Mark policy areas for counsel and jurisdiction validation                                       |
| AI agent architectural drift                                  |     Medium |   High | AGENTS.md, RULES.md, ADRs, tests, PR review                                                     |
| Portable course completion mistaken for site/task proficiency |     Medium |   High | Separate Story 3.4 and 5.4 gates; structured evidence; host competency confirmation (`ADR-017`) |
