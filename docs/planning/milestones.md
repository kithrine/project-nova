# Milestones

## M1 — Technical foundation complete
Authentication, environments, CI, database connectivity, and base layouts work.

## M2 — Applicant can apply
A participant can create, save, submit, and track an application.

## M3 — Operations can decide
Nova staff can complete eligibility, interview, background, and acceptance workflows.

## M4 — Participant becomes placement-ready
Enrollment, onboarding, training, and readiness blockers work.

## M5 — Shelter match approved
Nova can propose a match; participant and shelter can decide; approved match creates a placement.

## M6 — Placement activated
All prerequisites are enforced and the active placement workspace works.

## M7 — Hours and supervision work
Timesheets, evaluations, incidents, and case notes operate with proper permissions.

## M8 — Pilot reporting works
Nova can produce the minimum operational and funding reports required for the pilot.

## M9 — Production readiness
Accessibility, security, backup, observability, and operational launch checks pass.
