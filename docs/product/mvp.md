# MVP Scope

## Participant Portal

- Apply to Project Nova
- Save and resume application
- Upload required documents
- View application status and next step
- Complete onboarding tasks
- View placement details
- Record hours
- View certifications

## Nova Operations

- Applications queue
- Eligibility review
- Interview records
- Background decision tracking
- Acceptance and rejection
- Participant and enrollment records
- Onboarding and training
- Manual matching
- Placement management
- Lifecycle transitions
- Case notes
- Incidents
- Basic reports

## Shelter Portal

- Pending placement proposals
- Assigned participants
- Timesheet approval
- Evaluations
- Incident reporting
- Organization profile

## Deferred

- Messaging
- Advanced notifications
- Blended funding
- Payroll integrations
- Background-check integration
- Employer marketplace
- AI features
- Native mobile apps
