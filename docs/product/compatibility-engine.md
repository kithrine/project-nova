# Compatibility Engine

## Scope

MVP compatibility is coordinator-led decision support, not automated matching.

## Inputs

- Participant availability
- Required certifications
- Training completion
- Shelter capacity
- Site and supervisor availability
- Proposed schedule
- Transportation feasibility
- Placement start/end dates
- Any approved placement restrictions

## Output

The system displays:

- Compatible
- Potential concern
- Blocking incompatibility
- Unknown / needs review

## Rules

- The system never makes the final match decision.
- No opaque score should determine participant opportunity.
- Restricted background information is not displayed to shelters.
- Compatibility logic must be explainable.
- Any future AI recommendation remains advisory and human-approved.
