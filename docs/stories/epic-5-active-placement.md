# Epic 5 — Active Placement

## Goal
Operate an active transitional placement.

## Stories
1. Placement workspace.
2. Assign site, supervisor, and schedule.
3. Assign funding.
4. Placement onboarding.
5. Activation blockers.
6. Activate placement.
7. Pause and resume.
8. Complete, convert, withdraw, or terminate.
9. Case notes.
10. Evaluations.
11. Incidents.
