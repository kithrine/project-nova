# Data Governance

## Principles

- Collect only necessary data.
- Restrict sensitive data by role and scope.
- Preserve history.
- Audit sensitive access and exports.
- Separate production from nonproduction.
- Use synthetic data in testing.

## Retention

Exact periods require legal and grant review. Until defined, records must not be permanently deleted through ordinary workflows.

## Exports

Only named exports with approved fields are permitted.
