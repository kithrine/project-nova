# Pilot Operating Model

## Pilot concept

- Approximately 10 participants
- One or two shelter partners
- Approximately 12 weeks
- Prison dog-program graduates as preferred initial pipeline
- One active funding source per placement
- Nova or approved partner as employer of record

## Operational roles

- Program Coordinator
- Nova Administrator
- Shelter Manager
- Shelter Supervisor
- Payroll/employer partner
- Grant administrator

## Weekly rhythm

- Application and matching review
- Training and onboarding checks
- Timesheet deadlines
- Shelter check-ins
- Incident review
- Participant support
- Outcome tracking
