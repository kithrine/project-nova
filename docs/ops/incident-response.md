# Incident Response

## Categories

- Safety
- Injury
- Animal welfare
- Attendance
- Conduct
- Property
- Harassment
- Other

## Severity

- Minor
- Moderate
- Serious
- Emergency

## Rules

- Emergency form submission does not replace emergency services.
- Serious and emergency incidents immediately alert Nova Operations.
- Shelter users may report and add permitted follow-up.
- Nova Operations owns review and closure.
- Incident records are historical and not hard deleted.
- Sensitive details are restricted and excluded from general search.
